About ZackHomeCare

Compassionate care from a team you can trust

Our Story

Founded in 2015, ZackHomeCare began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, compassionate homecare services to Kenyan families at affordable prices. What started as a small team of three caregivers has grown into one of Nairobi's most trusted homecare providers, serving families across multiple counties.

Our founder, Dr. Zack Mwangi, recognized the growing need for professional in-home care services as Kenya's population ages and more families seek alternatives to institutional care. With his background in geriatric medicine, he established ZackHomeCare to bridge this gap with services that prioritize dignity, respect, and personalized attention.

Our Mission

To enhance the quality of life for our clients and their families by providing professional, personalized care that promotes independence, dignity, and well-being in the comfort of home.

Our Vision

To be Kenya's leading homecare provider, recognized for excellence in compassionate care and innovative care solutions that transform lives.

Our Values

Compassion

We treat every client with the kindness and respect we would want for our own family members.

Excellence

We maintain the highest standards of care through continuous training and quality improvement.

Integrity

We are honest, ethical, and transparent in all our interactions.

Respect

We honor the dignity, privacy, and cultural values of every individual we serve.
